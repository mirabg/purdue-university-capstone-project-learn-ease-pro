## Getting Started

### First Time Login

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**

   - Open your browser and go to the LearnEase Pro URL
   - You'll see the login screen with email and password fields

2. **Enter Your Credentials**

   - Email: Your institutional email address
   - Password: Your password (change default password on first login)

3. **Access Your Dashboard**
   - After successful login, you'll be redirected to your role-specific dashboard
   - Students ‚Üí Student Dashboard
   - Faculty ‚Üí Faculty Dashboard (if implemented)
   - Admins ‚Üí Admin Dashboard

### Registration (New Users)

1. Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"** on the login page
2. Fill in required information:
   - First Name and Last Name
   - Email Address (institutional email)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm Password
   - Phone Number (optional)
   - Address Information (optional)
3. Click **"Register"** to create your account
4. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

### Changing Your Password

1. Navigate to your profile settings
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password"

## Student Guide

### Accessing Your Dashboard

When you log in as a student, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

- **Welcome Message**: Personalized greeting with your name
- **Enrollment Statistics**: Number of courses you're enrolled in
- **Course Information**: Quick access to your courses
- **Recent Activity**: Latest updates and activities

### Browsing Courses

1. **Navigate to Courses**

   - Click "Courses" in the main navigation
   - View all available courses

2. **Search for Courses**

   - Use the search bar to find specific courses
   - Filter by course code, name, or instructor

3. **View Course Details**
   - Click on any course to see detailed information
   - View course description, instructor, and materials

### Enrolling in Courses

1. **Select a Course**

   - Browse or search for the course you want
   - Click on the course to view details

2. **Enroll**

   - Click the "Enroll" button
   - Your enrollment status will be "Pending" until approved
   - You'll receive notification when approved

3. **View Enrollment Status**
   - Check "My Enrollments" to see status:
     - **Pending**: Awaiting approval
     - **Accepted**: Enrollment confirmed
     - **Denied**: Enrollment not approved

### Accessing Course Materials

1. **Navigate to Your Course**

   - Go to "My Courses" or "Enrollments"
   - Click on an enrolled course

2. **View Materials**

   - See all uploaded materials (documents, videos, presentations)
   - Materials are organized by type:
     - üìÑ Documents (PDFs, Word files)
     - üé• Videos (MP4, etc.)
     - üìä Presentations (PowerPoint, slides)

3. **Download Materials**
   - Click on any material to download
   - Materials open in new tab or download automatically

### Participating in Discussion Boards

The ChatBoard feature allows you to engage with instructors and fellow students.

#### Viewing Discussions

1. **Access ChatBoard**

   - Click "Discussions" or "ChatBoard" in navigation
   - View all posts in chronological order

2. **Search Posts**

   - Use the search bar to find specific topics
   - Filter by keywords or author

3. **Navigate Pages**
   - Use pagination controls at the bottom
   - See post count and current page

#### Creating a Post

1. **Click "Create Post" or "New Post"**
2. **Fill in Post Details**:
   - **Title**: Brief, descriptive title (required)
   - **Content**: Your message or question (required)
3. **Submit**: Click "Post" or "Submit"
4. **View Your Post**: Appears immediately in the discussion board

#### Replying to Posts

1. **Open a Post**: Click on any post to view details
2. **Click "Reply"**
3. **Write Your Response**: Enter your reply text
4. **Submit**: Click "Post Reply"
5. **View Replies**: See all replies threaded under the original post

#### Editing Your Posts

1. **Find Your Post**: Navigate to the post you created
2. **Click "Edit"** (three-dot menu or edit button)
3. **Modify Content**: Update title or content
4. **Save**: Click "Save Changes"

#### Deleting Your Posts

1. **Find Your Post**
2. **Click "Delete"** (three-dot menu or trash icon)
3. **Confirm Deletion**: Confirm you want to delete
4. **Post Removed**: Post is permanently deleted

### Submitting Course Feedback

1. **Navigate to Course**

   - Go to the course you want to review
   - Click "Submit Feedback" or "Review"

2. **Rate the Course**

   - Select star rating (1-5 stars)
   - 1 star = Poor, 5 stars = Excellent

3. **Write Feedback**

   - Provide detailed comments
   - Be constructive and specific
   - Mention what worked well and what could improve

4. **Submit**
   - Click "Submit Feedback"
   - Your feedback is recorded (may be anonymous)

## Faculty Guide

### Managing Your Courses

#### Creating a New Course

1. **Navigate to Courses**

2. **Enter Course Information**:

   - **Course Code**: Unique identifier (e.g., CS101)
   - **Course Name**: Full course title
   - **Description**: Detailed course description
   - **Instructor**: Automatically set to you

#### Editing Course Details

1. **Find Your Course**: Navigate to your course list
2. **Click "Edit"**: Open the edit form
3. **Update Information**: Modify any course details

#### Deleting a Course

2. **Click "Delete"** (may require admin privileges)
3. **Confirm Deletion**: Confirm you want to delete

1. **Navigate to Your Course**

   - Documents (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
   - Videos (MP4, AVI, MOV)

4. **Choose File**:

   - Click "Choose File" or drag-and-drop
   - Select file from your computer

5. **Add Details** (optional):

   - Material name
   - Description
   - Order/sequence

6. **Upload**: Click "Upload" or "Save"

#### Organizing Materials

- Materials are automatically organized by type
- You can reorder materials if needed
- Update descriptions for clarity
- Delete outdated materials

### Managing Enrollments

1. **View Enrollment Requests**

   - Navigate to "Enrollments" or course-specific enrollments

2. **Review Student Information**

   - Click on enrollment to see student details
   - Review student profile if needed

3. **Approve or Deny**:

   - **Approve**: Click "Accept" or "Approve"
   - **Deny**: Click "Deny" with optional reason
   - Students are notified of decision

4. **View Enrolled Students**
   - See all accepted students in your course
   - View student count and list

### Moderating Discussions

#### Pinning Important Posts

1. **Navigate to ChatBoard**
2. **Find Important Post**
3. **Click "Pin Post"** (pin icon)
4. **Pinned Post**: Appears at top of discussions
5. **Unpin**: Click "Unpin" to remove from top

#### Managing Posts

- **Edit Posts**: Can edit any post in your course discussions
- **Delete Posts**: Can delete inappropriate content
- **Reply**: Respond to student questions
- **Monitor**: Keep discussions professional and on-topic

### Viewing Course Feedback

1. **Navigate to Course**
2. **Click "View Feedback"** or "Reviews"
3. **See Aggregated Ratings**:
   - Average star rating

   - Individual feedback comments
4. **Use Feedback**: Improve course based on student input

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Can't log in / "Invalid credentials"

**Solutions**:

1. Verify email address is correct
2. Check password (case-sensitive)
3. Use "Forgot Password" if needed
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try different browser
6. Contact admin if account is locked

**Problem**: Redirected to login after accessing protected page

**Solution**: Your session expired. Simply log in again.

### Course Enrollment Issues

**Problem**: Can't enroll in a course

**Possible Reasons**:

1. Already enrolled in the course
2. Course is full (max capacity)
3. Prerequisite not met
4. Account permissions issue

**Solutions**:

- Check "My Enrollments" to see if already enrolled
- Contact course instructor or admin
- Verify you meet prerequisites

**Problem**: Enrollment stuck in "Pending"

**Solution**:

- Enrollment requires approval from faculty/admin
- Contact the course instructor to expedite
- Allow 1-2 business days for processing

### ChatBoard Issues

**Problem**: Can't create a post

**Solutions**:

1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Check that both title and content are filled
3. Verify content doesn't exceed character limit
4. Refresh the page and try again
5. Clear browser cache

**Problem**: Can't edit or delete a post

**Reasons**:

- Only your own posts can be edited/deleted
- Admins/Faculty can moderate all posts
- Some posts may be locked after certain time period

**Problem**: Don't see reply option

**Solution**:

- Ensure you're logged in
- Click on the post to open full view
- Reply option should appear at bottom

### File Upload Issues

**Problem**: Course material won't upload

**Solutions**:

1. Check file size (max 10MB typically)
2. Verify file type is allowed:
   - Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX
   - Videos: MP4, AVI, MOV
   - Presentations: PPT, PPTX
3. Check internet connection
4. Try compressing large files
5. Contact admin if issue persists

**Problem**: Can't download course material

**Solutions**:

1. Ensure you're enrolled in the course
2. Check internet connection
3. Try right-click ‚Üí "Save Link As"
4. Contact instructor if file is missing

### Profile and Account Issues

**Problem**: Can't update profile information

**Solutions**:

1. Ensure all required fields are filled
2. Verify email format is correct
3. Check phone number format (xxx-xxx-xxxx)
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Contact admin for assistance

**Problem**: Forgot password

**Solution**:

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Follow instructions to reset password
5. If no email received, check spam folder
6. Contact admin if still unable to reset

### General Technical Issues

**Problem**: Page not loading / blank screen

**Solutions**:

1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache:
   - Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Cmd+Shift+Delete on Mac)
   - Select "Cached images and files"
   - Click "Clear data"
3. Try different browser
4. Check internet connection
5. Disable browser extensions temporarily
6. Contact support if issue persists

**Problem**: Slow performance

**Solutions**:

1. Close unnecessary browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check internet speed
4. Try during off-peak hours
5. Ensure browser is up to date

**Problem**: Features not working as expected

**Solutions**:

1. Make sure JavaScript is enabled
2. Update your browser to latest version
3. Try disabling ad blockers
4. Check if cookies are enabled
5. Try incognito/private browsing mode

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to your profile settings, click "Change Password", enter current and new password.

**Q: Can I enroll in multiple courses?**
A: Yes, there's no limit to the number of courses you can enroll in.

**Q: Are course materials downloadable?**
A: Yes, all course materials can be downloaded for offline access.

**Q: How do I know if my enrollment was accepted?**
A: Check "My Enrollments" for status updates, or you'll receive a notification.

### For Students

**Q: Can I unenroll from a course?**
A: Yes, go to "My Enrollments", find the course, and click "Unenroll" or contact admin.

**Q: Can I see course materials before enrolling?**
A: Typically no, materials are only accessible to enrolled students.

**Q: Can I edit my feedback after submitting?**
A: This depends on system configuration. Generally, feedback is final once submitted.

### For Faculty

**Q: How many courses can I create?**
A: No limit, create as many courses as you're teaching.

**Q: Can I have co-instructors?**
A: Currently, one instructor per course. Contact admin for multi-instructor needs.

**Q: Can I see who viewed my materials?**
A: Analytics features may be available - check with your admin.

### For Admins

**Q: Can I bulk import users?**
A: Check with technical team for CSV import capabilities.

**Q: How do I backup the system?**
A: Refer to technical documentation for backup procedures.

**Q: Can I customize user roles?**
A: Role customization requires technical configuration. See admin documentation.
